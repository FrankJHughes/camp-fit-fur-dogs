# ADR 0023 — Story Grammar as Canonical Story Format

## Status
Accepted

## Context
The previous planning system used Story YAML, sprint manifests, and automation scripts. These were removed in favor of a simpler, Markdown‑only story model.

## Decision
Adopt Story Grammar as the canonical story format for all product stories in product/stories/.

## Consequences
Stories are human‑readable, automation‑free, and aligned with GitHub Issues as tasks.
