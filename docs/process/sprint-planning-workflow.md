# Sprint Planning Workflow

## Story Selection Phase
- Stories may or may not have YAML.
- YAML is optional.
- Stories are evaluated on product merit, not planning metadata.

## Sprint Commitment Phase
- YAML is created for any selected story that lacks it.
- YAML is validated for readiness, dependencies, story points, and sprint assignment.

## Sprint Readiness
A sprint cannot be marked Ready unless:
- All stories have YAML.
- All planning metadata is complete.
- All dependencies are resolved.
