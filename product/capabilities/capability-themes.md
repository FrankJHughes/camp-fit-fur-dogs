# Capability Themes

## Identity & Ownership
The system knows who the customer is and what belongs to them.

## Dog Lifecycle Management
Customers can manage dogs from registration through removal.

## Safe Change & Recovery
Customers can make changes and recover from mistakes safely.

## Clarity & Guidance
Customers always understand what is happening and what to do next.

## Emotional Trust & Respect
The system treats customers as responsible, valued partners.
