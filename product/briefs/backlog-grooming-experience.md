# Backlog Grooming Experience — Product Brief

## Vision
Creating, refining, and validating stories is a frictionless, schema-safe
process that produces traceable, sprint-ready artifacts every time — no
hand-authoring YAML, no schema drift, and no orphaned specs.

## Principles
1. **Correct by construction.** Tooling guarantees schema compliance
   rather than relying on manual review or post-hoc validation.
2. **Atomic artifacts.** A product spec and its corresponding story
   artifact always ship together. One without the other is incomplete.
3. **Conventions are documented, not tribal.** Every naming pattern,
   directory convention, and lifecycle rule lives in a discoverable
   document — never in someone's head.

## Success Criteria
- Story creation takes seconds, not minutes
- Zero schema errors reach main — invalid stories are caught on PR
- Every story artifact traces to a product spec
- A new contributor can create a correctly-shaped story without asking
  how the planning system works
