# US-C15 — Understand Steps

## Intent

As a customer, I want to always understand where I am in a process and what comes next, so that I never feel lost or uncertain.

## Value

Clarity reduces anxiety. When a customer can see the shape of a workflow — where they are, what's left, and what just happened — they feel in control. Opacity breeds distrust.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Multi-step flows clearly indicate the current step and total steps
- Completed steps are visually confirmed
- The next expected action is always obvious
- The customer is never left on a screen with no clear path forward
- Transitions between steps feel predictable and intentional
