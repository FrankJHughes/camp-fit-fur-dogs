# US-042 — Safe Changes

## Intent

As a customer, I want to feel safe making changes to my information or my dog's information, knowing I can undo mistakes or that I'll be warned before something permanent happens.

## Value

Fear of making irreversible mistakes paralyzes customers. A system that communicates risk honestly and offers reversibility where possible empowers customers to act with confidence.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Changes that are reversible clearly communicate that they can be undone
- Changes that are irreversible clearly communicate that fact before execution
- The customer is never left guessing whether a change is permanent
- Undo or correction paths are available where technically feasible
- The emotional tone around changes is empowering, not cautionary

## Emotional Guarantees

- EG-06 Explicit risk and reversibility
