# US-039 — Information Safety

## Intent

As a customer, I want to feel confident that my personal information and my dog's information are handled carefully, so that I trust the system with sensitive details.

## Value

Trust is a prerequisite for engagement. Customers who feel their data is exposed or carelessly handled will disengage. The system must communicate safety through behavior, not just policy.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Personal and dog information is never displayed unnecessarily or to unauthorized viewers
- The system does not expose data in URLs, error messages, or logs visible to the customer
- The customer feels that information boundaries are respected throughout every interaction
- Sensitive actions involving personal data are communicated transparently
